Academic Foundation

The minimum requirements for diploma entrance.

  • done

    10th Standard Pass

    Must have passed the High School Leaving Certificate (HSLC) or equivalent examination from a recognized board.

  • done

    Core Subject Proficiency

    Mandatory subjects: Mathematics and Science. Minimum aggregate marks of 40% (General) or 35% (SC/ST) in these subjects.

Age Limit

Candidate should be between 15 and 20 years of age as of 1st July of the admission year. (Relaxation applicable for reserved categories).

Application Pathway

A streamlined 4-step process to secure your seat at Nowgong Polytechnic.

01

Registration

Create your account on the PAT (Polytechnic Admission Test) portal.

02

Entrance Test

Appear for the PAT examination conducted by DTE, Assam.

03

Choice Filling

Select Nowgong Polytechnic as your preferred institution.

04

Verification

Submit physical documents for authentication and fee payment.
